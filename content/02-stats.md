# Section 02 — Stats Bar (Harvey-style number impact)

## Section Label
```
Trusted by teams who need results
```

## Stats (3 large numbers)

| Number | Label |
|--------|-------|
| 24/7 | Always running |
| 500+ | Signals tracked daily |
| 10,000+ | Tasks completed monthly |

## Design Notes
- Full-width dark bar (slightly lighter than hero, e.g. #111113)
- Three numbers centered, large font (JetBrains Mono or Inter Bold)
- Numbers in accent color, labels in muted gray
- No border, seamless transition from hero
- Separator dots or vertical dividers between stats
